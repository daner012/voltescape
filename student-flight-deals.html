Student travel needs flexibility

Student flight deals are about stretching the budget without making the trip miserable. The best options usually involve flexible dates, carry-on packing and cities where food and transport are not too expensive.

Good student destinations

Athens, Budapest, Larnaca, Warsaw, Bucharest and some Italian cities can work well when fares and hotel prices line up.

How to keep the total price low

Watch baggage fees, airport transfers, local transport, food prices and hotel location. A cheap flight can become expensive if the city is not budget-friendly.

Voltescape tip

For students, compare total trip cost instead of ticket price only.
